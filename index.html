How we work

HOW WE DEAL ABOUT BUSINESS

NEX/BS Lite is a fully integrated suite of application designed to meet the inventory, sales & accounting needs of commercial companies.

Designed entirely using state-of-the art technologies and programming tools.

NEX/BS Lite provides high flexibility in operation and powerful reporting and enquiry capabilities that generate predictive and concise information.

The software can be operated on client-server environment and work on Windows 98/2000/XP. The database is your choice from Oracle, MS-SQL or ACCESS.

Exclusive Services

WE PROVIDE AWESOME SERVICES

NEX/BS Financials is an integrated suite of multi company financial system that makes your accounting tasks easier. Sales module support Bar Code scanning and barcode printing, handle multiple level of discounts on invoice, batch number and expiry date handling and many more. Inventory module handle multi-warehouse and multi-branches.

Emphasis has been placed upon providing concise management information for senior executives, whilst maintaining performance and ease of use. Customer specific security prevents unauthorized access to sensitive financial data. Flexible reports bring you all information and searching facility by keyword on the screen.

Modules within NEX/BS Lite can run stand alone or fully integrated. When the systems are integrated, the financial transaction the result from stock and sales will flow automatically through the system.
